+ More: how to read this

Fires = how many times this signal triggered in the full data history.

Win% = percentage of those fires where the signal was right (entry/add: price went up over next 30d; trim/exit: price went down over next 30d, meaning we got out at a good time).

Avg = average forward return 30 trading days after the signal.

A signal with low Fires (e.g. 1 or 2) is statistically meaningless — we just don't have enough data. Look for signals with at least 5 fires.

Green Win% (≥60%) = signal has historical edge on this ticker. Red Win% (<40%) = signal has historically failed on this ticker. Worth questioning before you act on it.
